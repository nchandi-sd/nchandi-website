Orientation

Are you interested in joining H&I? Service work through Hospital and Institutions is a rewarding way to spend your time. Being a panel member, you will go into facilities around San Diego carrying the message of hope and freedom to the still suffering alcoholic. To get started there's just a few things you need to do. Please use this page as a resource for the steps you need to take to become a panel member. We look forward to meeting you soon.

How-To's of Joining H&I


  1. Verify that you qualify based on the sobriety requirements below.
  2. Attend an orientation - see upcoming dates on this page.
  3. Go to our Panels page - find a panel you would like to join.
  4. Use contact finder to find the right person to speak to about joining the panel.

Sobriety Requirements

To participate in H&I you must meet the minimum sobriety requirement. While the committee never wants this to be a prohibitive factor that keeps someone from being of service, we kindly ask that you respect the minimum requirements.

  • Panel Visitors/Guests: 3 Months.
  • Panel Members: 6 months
  • Panel Leaders: 1 year
  • Panel Coordinator: 2 Years
