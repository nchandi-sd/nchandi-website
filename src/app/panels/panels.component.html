NCHANDI Panels

How-To's of Joining H&I

  1. Find an open panel on this page.
  2. Verify that you qualify based on the sobriety requirements below.
  3. Use contact finder to find person the right person to speak to about joining the panel.
  4. Attend an orientation

The Sobriety Requirements

  • Panel Coordinator: 2 Years
  • Panel Leaders: 1 year
  • Panel Members: 6 months
  • Panel Visitors/Guests: 3 Months.
