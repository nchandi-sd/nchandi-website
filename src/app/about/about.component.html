About Us

Who We Are:

    Formally known as the North County Area Institutional Committee (NCAIC), NCHANDI is a volunteer organization which organizes "Hospital and Institution" meetings of Alcoholics Anonymous. We help bring meetings into facilities in North San Diego county. Visit our resource area to learn more about how you can be of service.
