North County Hospitals and Institutions

Who we are:

    Formally known as the North County Area Institutional Committee (NCAIC), NCHANDI is a volunteer organization which organizes "Hospital and Institution" meetings of Alcoholics Anonymous. We help bring meetings into facilities in North San Diego county. If you are struggling with alcoholism we urge you to go to Alcoholics Anonymous and find a meeting near you. If you are looking to be of service in A.A. and live in North County San Diego then you are in the right place.

    Visit our resource area to learn more about how you can be of service.
